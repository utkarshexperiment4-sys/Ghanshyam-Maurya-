Taaji Sabziyan • Fresh From Our Fields

Har subah, khet se seedha taazi sabzi tor kar laaye jaati hai. Koi cold storage nahi, koi lambe time ka stock nahi – jo aaj katega, wahi aaj bikta hai.

  • Taaji Sabziyan: Palak, Methi, Saag, Bhindi, Lauki, Tori, Tamatar, Aloo aur aur bhi.
  • 100% Organic: Koi chemical fertilizer ya pesticide ka istemaal nahi – sirf gomutra, khaad, aur prakritik tareeke.
  • Mitti ki sehat, paani ki bachat aur prakriti ki suraksha hamari sabse badi zimmedari hai.
